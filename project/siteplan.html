Rayitos de Sol

Daycare for children ages 1 to 3

Site Name

Rayitos de Sol represents the joy, warmth, and energy of young children. It is a friendly and easy-to-remember name for parents.

Suggested domain: rayitosdesol.com

Site Purpose

The website will provide information about the facilities, staff, and the type of education offered at the daycare. In addition, it will allow parents to schedule a visit through a contact form (WhatsApp or Instagram).

Scenarios

  • Who are the teachers and what experience do they have?
  • What are the facilities like and what safety measures are in place?
  • What kind of educational activities are carried out with the children?
  • How can I schedule a visit to get to know the daycare?

Color Scheme

  • Yellow (#FFE066): For headings and accents.
  • Blue (#0057AD): For backgrounds and highlighted sections.
  • Light gray (#F7F7F7): General background.

Typography

  • Montserrat: For titles and headings.
  • Open Sans: For body text.
